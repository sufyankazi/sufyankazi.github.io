The only EcoVillage for $0 down.

An intentional community by Crosby Islamic Center

We believe a natural way of living and a strong community don't have to be expensive

Here, luxurious nature surrounds with abundant fruit trees, acres of field and forest, and an established future. Community members are planning today so that our children tomorrow have the support, health, and freedom to choose their way in life. By strategically sharing our cost of living, our labor, and our laughter we can build a healthy home without sacrificing privacy or requiring a large down payment - or any down payment ;).

Our community is located just 30 minutes from downtown Houston and offers over 25 acres of total property space. We're opening up only five spots in total for a select group of interested applicants. If you're interested in earthbags, strawbale homes, earthships, or sustainable living, come find a home with us.

You may be a great fit if:

  • You like to work with your hands.
  • You enjoy the outdoors.
  • Learning doesn’t scare you.

We're agile - that means we don't enforce a specific model.

If you'd like to bring a big chunk of cash, welcome! Otherwise, we're proposing a model where you build your own earth home or tiny house on wheels on community land. That means you'll get the pros of home ownership without the cons.

You'll pay rent on the land (starting at $600/mo) which will cover a portion of utilities, taxes, mortgage payments, and more. In exchange, you get your own private site (up to 2 acres) with access to additional community space. None of the money paid in rent is profit so you can rest assured that you're paying your fair share and nothing more. Next, as a community member you'll have a fair vote along with other members on major decisions and financials.

We are realistic and fair minded, so please bring your concerns to us and will make sure to accommodate any reasonable requests.
