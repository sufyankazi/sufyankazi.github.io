Welcome to Crosby Islamic Center!


With our land acquired in 2022, we began the project of making the first masjid in Crosby with great hopes to support and grow the local muslim population. We center our goals of education, sustainability, and community around the worship of God. With his help, we hope that this 25+ acre property becomes the home for muslims all around and serves the surrounding community. We welcome all people to come say hello.

Currently, we are planning the mosque and reaching out to locals. Please give us a call or text below to schedule a visit or if you'd just like to chat!
